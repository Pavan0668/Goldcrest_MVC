Infrastructure

At Goldcrest High, Latur, students have access to the best resources and amenities that keep them safe, secured and valued. The infrastructure at Latur enhances the overall learning outcome and academic achievements of students.

Smart classrooms

When technology is incorporated into regular lessons, students are sure to become interested as it replaces the old traditional repetitive and boring methods with a a more engaging and effective environment.

Library

The Primary and Senior sections have separate well equipped libraries with open book shelves for easy access.

Science Labs

The large well-equipped Biology, Physics, Maths and Chemistry labs with modern amenities and quality specimens and models, open up a world for children to observe, experiment and comprehend. ‘Learning by Doing’ remains the motto.

Computer Labs

With computer education an essential part of modern day living, it has become the priority of the school to make students and teachers computer savvy. Special courses on animation coding, graphic designing etc., apart from the regular syllabus, are conducted.

Discover

Welcome to an Educational Institution that aims to set a benchmark for others! Goldcrest High has been founded under the aegis of Vilasrao Deshmukh Foundation (VDF) established in 2010, under the leadership of the visionary and philanthropist, Honourable Shri Vilasrao Deshmukh.

Vision

Our goal is to offer integrated value education that cultivates global insight while preserving Indian culture, empowering students to excel in every facet of life.

Mission

Our mission is to mold students into compassionate, brave, and competent global citizens. We aim to deliver an education that not only ensures the prosperous future of our children but also equips them with lifelong assets.

Philosophy

Goldcrest High believes in providing children with an environment that integrates individual and group learning, knowledge, values, reasoning and creativity into a seamless mix of experiences, discoveries and excitement. Goldcrest High is designed to be a true centre of learning, a place where students enjoy the thrill of learning new things each day. Here learning is not an imposition but an excitement and the key to a greater freedom than they have ever known.

Inspired by Rabindranath Tagore's vision for an educated community, Goldcrest High imbibes the spirit of creativity and co-curricular excellence that complements the academic learning beautifully.

Goldcrest High aims at providing an education that promotes intellectual freedom, teaches children to live in harmony and to appreciate the diversity of our cultures and communities.
