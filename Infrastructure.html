Infrastructure

At Goldcrest High, Latur, students have access to the best resources and amenities that keeps them safe, secured and valued. The high quality infrastructure at Latur enhances the overall learning outcome and academic achievements of students.

Smart classrooms

When technology is incorporated into regular lessons, students are sure to become interested as it creates a more engaging and effective environment rather than old traditional repetitive and boring methods.

Library

The school has separate well equipped libraries for the Primary and Senior sections. The libraries have open book shelves for easy access.

Science Labs

The school has huge well-equipped Biology, Physics, Maths and Chemistry Labs. The labs with modern amenities and quality specimens and models, open up a world for children to observe, experiment and comprehend. ‘Learning by Doing ‘remains the motto.

Computer Labs

With computer education an essential part of modern day living, it has become the priority of the school to make students and teachers, computer savvy. Special courses on animation coding, graphic designing etc. apart from regular syllabus, are imparted.

Sports Ground

The school playground is a multisport ground equipped with artificial turf. The facility is of an international standard which facilitates sports like athletics, basketball, volleyball, cricket, football etc. We encourage students to participate in sports activities for enjoyment, relaxation and exercise.

Art Room

The Art Room Stands vibrant and bright. It is a sanctuary where creativity takes flight. It is a space where young talented are beautifully engaged with Brushes, Paints, and canvases.

Multi-Purpose Hall

Multi-purpose hall is used for school assemblies, parent orientations, lesson enrichment programs and to facilitate indoor games and physical fitness programs.

Cafeteria

There is a canteen facility for the students. The food served in the canteen is hygienic and healthy.

CCTV Surveillance

The entire campus is under CCTV surveillance to ensure a safe learning environment.

Infirmary

The school is equipped with an infirmary. A qualified and experienced nurse is available on campus to manage and assess any health issues that may arise during school hours.

Admissions open for Pre-primary to Grade 9th (ICSE) and 11th (ISC) for the academic year 2024-25
