About Us

Welcome to an Educational Institution that aims to set a benchmark for others!
Goldcrest High has been founded under the aegis of Vilasrao Deshmukh Foundation (VDF) established in 2010, under the leadership of the visionary and philanthropist, Honourable Shri Vilasrao Deshmukh.

The school looks to providing an education that promotes intellectual freedom, teaches children to live in harmony and to appreciate the diversity of our cultures and communities. The school's excellent facilities are designed to enhance the academic and physical development of children. The staff is committed to developing global citizens who will make a difference to society and eventually the world.

This education system will develop within the child the full range of human capacities - intellectual, social, physical, emotional, and spiritual. It will instill a thirst for knowledge and foster a desire for excellence.

In a nutshell, the aim of Goldcrest High is to evolve into a true Learning Centre where students are empowered to fly 'on the wings of excellence'.

Vision

Our goal is to offer integrated value education that cultivates global insight while preserving Indian culture, empowering students to excel in every facet of life.

Mission

Our mission is to mold students into compassionate, brave, and competent global citizens. We aim to deliver an education that not only ensures the prosperous future of our children but also equips them with lifelong assets.

Philosophy

Goldcrest High believes in providing children with an environment that integrates individual and group learning, knowledge, values, reasoning and creativity into a seamless mix of experiences, discoveries and excitement. Goldcrest High is designed to be a true centre of learning, a place where students enjoy the thrill of learning new things each day. Here learning is not an imposition but an excitement and the key to a greater freedom than they have ever known.

Inspired by Rabindranath Tagore's vision for an educated community, Goldcrest High imbibes the spirit of creativity and co-curricular excellence that complements the academic learning beautifully.

Founder’s Note

Goldcrest High imparts quality education keeping in mind the total development of the child.

We endeavour to design our educational processes on the basis that the future of this world rests upon the overall competence, sensibility and goodness of its youth. The need of the hour is to raise our children in such a way that they lead a good quality life and become responsible global citizens of the future.

We thus have to educate them not only with instruction but also by drawing out their hidden potential with all the attention and care that they deserve.

The purpose of providing quality education is manifold. Apart from making them more knowledgeable and good problem solvers, it enables students to live their life fully at all ages, prepares them for challenges ahead and grooms them in noble culture values. Still higher than this aim is that it creates in students a thirst for greater knowledge.

Goldcrest High imparts this quality education keeping in mind the total development of the child. Children get a modern education in an atmosphere that preserves the rich Indian culture, values achievement, builds character and enriches these young minds. Most of all the school enshrines the dignity and self-worth of each individual.

Honourable Shri. Vilasrao Deshmukh

From The Principal's Desk

Dear Parents,

Welcome to the Goldcrest High family!

At Goldcrest High, we believe education is more than just filling minds with facts. It is about igniting intelligence, shaping character, and fostering imagination. As Margaret Mead said, "Children must be taught how to think, not what to think," and that's exactly what we strive to do.

Every child, from the "weak" to the "gifted," is challenged and nurtured to reach their full potential. We understand that the world is evolving rapidly and our focus is to equip students with the skills and values they need to thrive in this dynamic landscape.

While education today often emphasizes technology and globalization, we never lose sight of the importance of moral values and principles. Aristotle eloquently stated, "Educating the mind without educating the heart is no education at all." At Goldcrest High, we instill these fundamental qualities alongside academic excellence, shaping our students into responsible, sensitive citizens of the future.

Our curriculum, from primary to high school, is dynamic and responsive to the changing needs of the world. We emphasize hands-on experiences, collaborative activities, and a spirit of discovery. Beyond academics, our co-curricular activities spark creativity and imagination through art, music, dance, sports, and yoga. We recognize the importance of emotional well-being, and our open and interactive learning environment fosters confidence and resilience.

At Goldcrest High, teamwork is our foundation. Together, we create a nurturing space where every child can blossom. We believe our students are the future leaders, and we are dedicated to providing them with the tools and experiences they need to navigate the challenges and embrace the opportunities that lie ahead.

Usha Kiran Sood

Principal
